Welcome to the Hostel Complaints System, where students can easily report issues related to their living environment. Whether you're facing general concerns about noise or policies, experiencing electrical problems like faulty outlets or lights, needing carpenter assistance for broken furniture or fixtures, or requiring cleaning services for unsanitary shared spaces, we’re here to help. Please select the relevant category below to submit your complaint, and rest assured that our team is dedicated to addressing your concerns promptly for a better hostel experience.

Cleaning

Students often report issues with cleanliness in shared spaces like bathrooms, corridor. Complaints may include unemptied trash bins, dirty floors, or unclean dishes in communal areas. Some might express concerns about the lack of regular cleaning schedules or inadequate sanitization, especially in high-touch areas

Electricity

Electrical issues can lead to frustration among students. Common complaints include malfunctioning outlets, flickering lights, or issues with the Wi-Fi connection. Some may experience problems with air conditioning or heating units, leading to discomfort. Guests often request prompt repairs to ensure a safe and comfortable stay.

Carpenter

Furniture complaints may arise from items being damaged, uncomfortable, or lacking in functionality. This could include broken beds, insufficient seating in common areas, or inadequate storage solutions in dorm rooms. Students often express concerns about the overall quality and comfort of the furnishings, which can impact their overall experience.

General

General complaints encompass a wide range of issues, including noise disturbances from other students, inadequate LAN connectivity. Students may also express concerns about the hostel’s policies or staff behavior, which can influence their perception of the overall environment.
