HALL OF RESIDENCE

NIT Warangal is a fully residential campus and provides accommodation facilities to all the students admitted to various courses. The Institute has thirteen halls of Residence for boys and three for girls. The halls of Residence administration is managed by the Chief Warden along with the team of Wardens and Assistant Wardens. The Warden is the administrative head of each hall of Residence who will be assisted by Assistant Wardens. The Wardens will form the halls committee comprising the students who will act as Maintenance Secretary, Environment Secretary, Cultural Secretary and Games & Sports Secretary. The committee decides the pattern and the pace of life of each Hall.


The Halls of Residence Advisory Council is the apex decision making body concerning the Halls of Residence which is headed by the Director and is responsible for major policy decisions of the Hall of Residence administration.


The Hall Disciplinary Committee (HDC) will look after any act of indiscipline in a Hall of residence and will be investigated by a Hall Disciplinary Committee which will recommend the action to the Warden of the Hall. He /she will decide the course of action to be taken and implement it. However, in case the matter is of a serious nature, the warden will forward it to the Dean (SW) through the Chief Warden. The Dean (SW) who is the Chairman of the ISDC will take necessary action.
